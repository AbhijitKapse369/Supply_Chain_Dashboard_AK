# Supply Chain Performance Dashboard
**Tools:** Excel | Tableau Public | PivotTables | COUNTIFS | Data Visualization

## Project Overview
Analyzed 9,999 global supply chain orders across 5 markets to identify 
late delivery patterns, shipping inefficiencies, and profit-discount 
relationships.

## Key Insights
- **53.7% late delivery rate** across all markets
- **Europe worst-performing** market at 60.1% late deliveries
- **First Class shipping** has 99.65% late delivery despite premium pricing
- **Second Class** highest avg delay at 2.01 days
- **Higher discounts = lower profit** confirmed via scatter trend analysis
- **Apparel & Golf** top revenue-generating departments

## Dashboard Features
### Excel Dashboard
- 6 PivotCharts with dynamic slicers (Market, Shipping Mode, Segment, Department)
- KPI cards: Total Orders, Revenue, Profit, Late Delivery Rate, Avg Delay
- Analysis sheet with business questions and live metrics

### Tableau Dashboard
- Geographic delivery map (late deliveries by country)
- Late delivery by market (bar chart)
- Monthly late delivery trend (line chart)
- Avg shipping delay by mode
- Revenue by department (treemap)
- Profit vs discount rate by segment (scatter + trend lines)
